# Baby Shark with Jaws Intro

**Artist**: Pinkfong
**Genre**: Children's pop / novelty
**Selected because**: Extremely simple, repetitive beat structure with a clear children's-song cadence. The Jaws intro prelude provides a contrasting low-energy instrumental section before the high-energy main theme begins, giving the harness a multi-section song where section transitions are unambiguous.

## What the pro did well

- Beat-lock is tight throughout — effects snap cleanly to each "do do do do do" phrase boundary
- The Jaws intro is treated as a distinct section (tense, minimal effects) that yields to a burst of energy at the first "Baby Shark" hit
- Upper-tier heroes activate predictably on each chorus repetition rather than staying on continuously

## Caveats

- Song has essentially no harmonic complexity; chord-change metrics will score near zero and are not meaningful benchmarks for this entry
- The looping, repetitive structure means section-boundary precision matters more than theme diversity — a sequencer that assigns the same theme to every section is still correct behavior here
