# Candy Cane Lane

**Artist**: Sia
**Genre**: Christmas pop
**Selected because**: Mid-tempo Christmas pop with a clear verse/chorus/bridge structure and Sia's characteristically wide dynamic range. Good test of theme contrast between understated verses and anthemic choruses.

## What the pro did well

- Verse sections use restrained, colour-wash style effects that let the atmospheric intro breathe
- Chorus sections escalate clearly: upper tiers activate, palette shifts to warm reds and greens
- Bridge breakdown is handled with a brief sparse moment before the final chorus payoff
- Beat emphasis on the snare hits in the chorus is consistent

## Caveats

- Sia's vocal production pushes a lot of energy into the mid-high frequency range; onset detectors that rely on low-frequency transients may miss some beat emphasis moments
- The song uses a rubato intro before the click track locks in — sequences that chase the free-tempo intro may show phase drift in the first 10 seconds
