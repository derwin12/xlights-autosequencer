**Genre**: Christmas pop / upbeat
**Selected because**: High-energy, consistently upbeat Christmas track with a straightforward verse/chorus/bridge structure and strong rhythmic emphasis throughout. - Upper hero tiers remain active for most of the song, appropriate to the relentlessly upbeat character of the track
